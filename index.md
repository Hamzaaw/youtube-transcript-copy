# Privacy Policy

**YouTube Transcript Copy** is a Chrome extension that allows users to copy YouTube video transcripts directly to their clipboard. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and only interacts with the content of the currently active YouTube tab when activated.

## Permissions

The extension uses the following permissions:

- `activeTab`: To access the content of the currently active YouTube page.
- `clipboardWrite`: To copy the transcript to your clipboard.
- `scripting`: To inject a script that retrieves the transcript.

These permissions are used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension. No data is transmitted outside of your device.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository’s Issues tab.
